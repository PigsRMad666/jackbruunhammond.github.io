About

A bit
about me

I'm Jack Bruun-Hammond, a graphic designer and marketing coordinator based in Sydney. I work across brand, editorial, digital and UX/UI — currently at the University of Sydney, with a long run of freelance and DIY projects behind me.

I'm self-taught first and formally trained second. I tend to learn whatever a project needs — design, web, video, a bit of code, lately 3D — and build from there. A lot of the work grows out of music: twenty years playing in punk and hardcore bands, and that DIY instinct runs through everything I make.

I move comfortably between clean, restrained design and the more broken, expressive end of things — and I like picking the right one for the job.
